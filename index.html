Kaldereta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef(cubed)
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves crushed and chopped
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ½ cup liver spread processed using blender
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • ⅔ cup green olives
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the beef and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  2. Step 2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and softened.
  3. Step 3. Add the tomato sauce, liver spread (if using), and soy sauce to the pot. Stir until well combined.
  4. Step 4. Return the browned beef to the pot. Add water or beef broth and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender. (For faster cooking, use a pressure cooker and cook for 30-40 minutes.)
  5. Step 5. Add the potatoes and carrots to the pot. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until slightly tender. Stir in the bell peppers, green peas, and chilies (if using). Cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked but still vibrant.
  6. Step 6. Add vinegar, sugar, grated cheese, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir until the cheese has melted and the flavors are well blended.
  7. Step 7. Transfer the kaldereta to a serving dish. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
